Common Questions About Recruitment

  • How can I apply for a position?
    You can apply through our official email address (recruit@metarock.co.kr) or through external job search platforms.
  • What is the recruitment scale and timing?
    We typically hire around XX new employees annually and recruit year-round.
  • What is the recruitment process?
    The process consists of: 1) Document screening, 2) Team leader interview, 3) Executive interview.
  • Is there an age requirement?
    Candidates must be at least 20 years old. We do not discriminate based on education, gender, or ethnicity.
  • What benefits do employees receive?
    We offer statutory insurance, 5-day work week, monthly leave, incentives, stock options, and special occasion leave.
  • Can soon-to-be graduates apply?
    Yes, you can submit a certificate of expected graduation initially and provide the degree certificate later.
  • What documents should I prepare?
    You need a brief self-introduction, resume, and if applicable: graduation certificate, degree certificate, and experience certificate.
